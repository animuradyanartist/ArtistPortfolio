Ani Muradyan is an Armenian contemporary painter working between abstract realism and poetic expression.
Using oil on canvas and mixed media on paper, she creates emotionally resonant works that explore hope, vulnerability, memory, and inner strength. Through expressive color, layered textures, and flowing forms, her paintings balance fragility and resilience.
Portraiture, symbolic imagery, and atmospheric landscapes come together in a visual language that feels both intimate and timeless.

Based in Armenia, Ani Muradyan creates contemporary art for collectors seeking original paintings with depth, warmth, and emotional presence. Her works are designed not only to be seen, but to be felt, bringing reflection, serenity, and meaning into a space.
